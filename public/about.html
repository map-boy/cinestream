About CineStream

CineStream is a modern movie and TV discovery platform built by VAF Ubwenge Tech, a student-led technology company based in Kigali, Rwanda.

Our mission is to build practical, high-quality software products across web, mobile, and AI. CineStream showcases our work in frontend engineering, API integration, and user experience design, using real-world movie and TV metadata from The Movie Database (TMDB).

What We Do

VAF Ubwenge Tech builds websites, mobile applications, and AI-powered tools, with a particular focus on solving problems relevant to Rwanda and the wider region, including language technology for Kinyarwanda.
